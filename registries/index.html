Donation Alternative

We believe that our wedding is about more than just us. We have been blessed beyond measure with gifts, talents, financial means, and one another! One of our highest aims in our marriage is to help one another to use these blessings to bless others.

For this reason, we are partnering with World Vision, a Christian humanitarian organization dedicated to improving the lives of people all over the world, regardless of religion, racial, or ethnic boundaries. World Vision seeks to deal with the root causes of poverty and injustice by meeting physical needs of communities in a sustainable way. We support their vision, and we invite you to join with us!

How this works

Please consider making a donation to World Vision on our behalf before, during, or after the wedding in lieu of a traditional wedding gift. We will fully match every gift made in honor of our marriage.

To donate online at any time, go to our online giving portal to make the gift of your choice. If you prefer to donate at the wedding itself, we will be collecting donations at the reception (checks made out to World Vision will receive a tax deductible receipt).

Donations can also be given by mail to World Vision at P.O. Box 9716, Federal Way, WA 98063 or by phone at 1-888-511-6519. If donating by mail or phone, please indicate our Mead Wedding Fund source code number (118515257) so we can track your donation.

We have faith that our combined financial efforts can have a lasting impact on our world. Thanks for partnering with us!
