The Chapel at Red Rocks
1:30 pm

We have chosen The Chapel at Red Rocks as the place where we will be married! Bring your camera, as the chapel is known for its astounding views of Red Rocks Park and the Rocky Mountain front range.

The Chapel at Red Rocks is located at 905 Bear Creek Avenue, Morrison, Colorado. Take C-470 to Morrison Road and travel west for less than a mile. You will see the sign for the chapel on the right.
